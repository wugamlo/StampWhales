Welcome to the StampWhales Collection by Wugamlo!

StampWhales - Unprunable 24x24 pixel UTXO Artworks on Bitcoin

The Whales were stamped relatively early after the Bitcoin stamping was discovered and therefore have low stamp numbers. All assets are locked and the supply is fixed. Open Counterparty dispensers can be found with the links to the Xchain Explorer.

The Artwork was designed exclusively in MS Paint and many of the traits are inspired by the famous CryptoPunk collection.
